I designed (and fabricated) the exhaust system on the 2004 and 2005 cars. An exhaust system requires certain tube lengths for proper tuning and one of the biggest challenges of exhaust system design is meeting the performance requirements while meeting geometric constraints set by the chassis, suspension and other critical systems.

The 2005 car with the exhaust system visable. This was a similar design to the previous year's with a few minor changes based on lessons learned in 2004.
